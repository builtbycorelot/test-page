Master Email Template

Brand-compliant HTML email template for all CORELOT communications

Professional Email Foundation

Our master email template is a professionally coded HTML email designed to work across all major email clients. It features responsive design for mobile devices, our brand colors and typography, and accessible markup that ensures every recipient has a great experience.

This template serves as the foundation for newsletters, property listings, client updates, and all email communications from CORELOT Homes.

Key Features

Mobile-Responsive Design

Automatically adjusts for smartphones and tablets. Over 60% of emails are opened on mobile—we ensure yours look great everywhere.

Brand-Compliant Styling

CORELOT orange (#cc5500), Calibri typography, and professional layout maintain brand consistency in every message.

Cross-Client Compatibility

Works consistently in Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, and other email clients using proven HTML techniques.

How to Use This Template

1. Copy the HTML into your email marketing platform or HTML editor.

2. Customize content: Replace placeholders with your headline, images, body copy, and call-to-action.

3. Test thoroughly: Send test emails to multiple clients (Gmail, Outlook, mobile) before sending to your list.

4. Personalize when possible: Use merge tags for recipient names to increase engagement.

Email Best Practices

  • Clear subject lines: Under 50 characters, relevant, create curiosity without being misleading
  • One primary goal: Each email should have one main action you want recipients to take
  • Scannable content: Short paragraphs, bullet points, subheadings, plenty of white space
  • Professional images: Use our professional photography and renderings
  • Strong CTAs: Clear, action-oriented button text like "View Floor Plans" or "Schedule a Tour"
