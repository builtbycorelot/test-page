Single Home Listing Email

Email template for promoting individual home listings with photos, features, and pricing

Overview

Our single home email template is designed specifically for marketing individual properties—whether spec homes, model homes, or unique custom builds. This template highlights property photos, key features, floor plans, pricing, and includes strong calls-to-action to drive showings and inquiries.

When to Use

Use this template when marketing a specific completed or near-complete home. Perfect for new listings, price reductions, open house invitations, or highlighting a special property to your email list.

Template Elements

• Hero image of the home exterior • Property address and key specs (beds/baths/sq ft) • Photo gallery section • Feature highlights and finishes • Floor plan image • Pricing and financing information • Strong CTA buttons (Schedule Tour, Request Info) • Agent contact information
