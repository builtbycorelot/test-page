Spot Lot Marketing Flyer

Print-ready flyer template for individual building lots

Professional Lot Marketing

Our spot lot flyer is a one-page, print-ready template designed for marketing individual building lots. Perfect for yard signs, open houses, direct mail, or realtor outreach, this flyer includes all essential information buyers need to evaluate a lot opportunity.

What's Included

  • Lot address and parcel information
  • Lot size (acres or square footage)
  • Dimensions and simple site map
  • Zoning and permitted uses
  • Utility availability status
  • Pricing and financing options
  • Contact information with QR code
  • Professional lot photography
  • CORELOT branding and Equal Housing logo

Best Uses

Yard signs: Weather-resistant flyer boxes on for-sale signs
Open houses: Handouts for interested buyers
Direct mail: Targeted mailings to prospective lot buyers
Realtor outreach: Leave with agents who work with buyers
